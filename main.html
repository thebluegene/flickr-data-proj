7 Years of Photography

For over 7 years now photography

For these several visualisations, I asked myself the following questions:

  • How has my photography changed over time?
  • When do I take most of my photos?
  • What have been my most common settings and equipment to use?
  • How much has my posting frequency changed?

I like to think that my photography style has evolved significantly, with major shifts marked by my attempts at 365 projects. I honestly thought there would be more discernable differences betrween the dominant colors.
